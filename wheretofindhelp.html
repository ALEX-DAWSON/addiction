Amphetamines

Maya Kalan-McPhee

This section of our article will be based on a multitude of different options for treatment based on the side effects of each drug. The first drug I am going to be touching on in Amphetamines. Amphetamine is a very strong and pricey drug. From the research I've done I have noticed that some of the drugs are a little bit more costly than others. Side effects can lead to death, hallucinations and massive migraines. All of these side effects can be dealt with if you get help.For specifically Amphetamines some of the things you can do is do a detoxification. This means that you would almost be doing something similar to a food cleanse but with drugs. This is an extremely long and difficult process so if you're struggling with an addiction you need to be prepared that this won't happen instantly. Artemis states that the detox pathway is “Detoxification, or “detox” for short, is the body’s process of removing toxic substances. Detoxification is not a food, or diet, or a pill - it is an ongoing, VITAL process that your body does (all on its own) in order to survive. It happens 24/7, 365 days of the year. Not just for a few days or weeks prescribed to “do a detox”.Your main detoxification organs are the liver and kidneys. The bowels and urinary tract are extensions of these organs as wastes exit your body.”
