Opioids

Symptoms for opioid withdrawals include runny nose, teary eyes, restlessness, insomnia, uncontrollable yawning, excessive sweating, anxiety, and muscle spasms. Some more intense symptoms are rapid heart rate, high blood pressure, dilated pupils, perinioya, vomiting, and diarrhea. To help cope with these withdrawal symptoms you can take hot baths, exercise, yoga, do stuff that entertains you, and keep hydrated.
